# PROOF THE HEDGEHOG — MASCOT ILLUSTRATION BRIEF
**ProofLabs Brand Mascot • Design Specifications v1.0**

---

## THE CHARACTER

**Name:** Proof the Hedgehog  
**Role:** ProofLabs brand mascot, appears throughout site as guide/personality  
**Tagline:** The "Proof" in "ProofLabs"

### Core Specifications

**Species:** Hedgehog  
**Color:** Golden/amber matching site primary color
- Body base: `#E8913A`
- Highlight/belly: `#F5BC6E`
- Lab coat: `#FFFFFF` with `#E8E0D8` shadows
- Eyes: `#2C2420` (dark, expressive)
- Nose: `#C77520`
- Spine tips: `#C77520` fading to `#E8913A` at base

**Proportions:** Small and round. Cute, not realistic.
- Think Pusheen or Molang proportions
- Stubby limbs, big head, minimal detail
- Round body, short legs
- Large expressive eyes

**Signature Item:** Tiny white lab coat (always worn)

**Eyes:** Large, round, dark — highly expressive. Can convey:
- Curiosity
- Excitement
- Pride
- Confusion
- Sleepiness
- Analytical focus

---

## STYLE GUIDE

**Art Style:** 2D flat illustration
- Clean lines, minimal shading
- Flat color fills with ONE shade for depth (no gradients in character)
- Hand-drawn feel but clean execution
- NOT corporate vector art
- NOT children's book detailed

**Technical Requirements:**
- Must work at 48px (small icon)
- Must work at 120px (medium callout avatar)
- Must work at 400px (large hero illustration)
- Must work on white AND cream backgrounds
- Transparent background
- SVG vector + PNG exports

**Visual References:**
- **Duolingo's owl** — Personality, expressiveness, consistent across contexts
- **Pusheen** — Round proportions, simple, cute
- **Notion's illustrations** — Clean, minimal, effective at small sizes
- **Mailchimp's Freddie** — Simple, recognizable silhouette, works as an icon

## CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS

**Critical:** All 20 poses must share:
- Same body proportions
- Same color palette
- Same level of detail
- Same line weight
- Same style treatment
- Recognizable as same character from silhouette alone

**Character Identity:**
- Round, friendly, approachable
- Smart but not stuffy (the lab coat is playful, not serious)
- Expressive eyes are the personality center
- Minimal mouth detail (eyes do the expression work)
- Spines should be simple - not individually drawn, more like a textured edge

**Lab Coat Details:**
- Should be miniature/cute (like a kid playing scientist)
- Slightly oversized on the character is fine
- Can have tiny pockets
- Can have 2-3 tiny buttons
- Should not overwhelm the character design

## ART DIRECTION NOTES FOR ILLUSTRATOR

**DO:**
- Keep it simple and clean
- Make eyes large and expressive
- Use flat colors with minimal shading
- Make spines stylized (not realistic/prickly)
- Keep lab coat cute and playful
- Test at small sizes (48px must be recognizable)
- Ensure poses read clearly at a glance

**DON'T:**
- Add gradients or complex shading
- Make realistic/detailed
- Add textures beyond flat color
- Overcomplicate the spines
- Make lab coat look professional/serious
- Add busy backgrounds
- Create poses that don't scale down

## TIMELINE & BUDGET GUIDANCE

**Recommended Approach:**
- Create 10 core poses first (priority 1)
- Review and approve style
- Complete remaining 10 secondary poses
- Deliver all files in batch

**Estimated Scope:**
- Core 10 poses: ~8-12 hours design time
- Secondary 10 poses: ~6-8 hours design time
- File exports and prep: ~2 hours
- **Total:** 16-22 hours design work

**Budget Range (Fiverr/99designs):**
- Lower end: $300-500 (overseas illustrator)
- Mid-range: $600-1000 (experienced character designer)
- High end: $1200-1800 (professional with mascot portfolio)

**Alternative: AI Generation**
- Use this brief as Midjourney/DALL-E prompt
- Will require manual cleanup and consistency work
- Cost: $30-50 for generation + $200-400 for cleanup/vectorization
- Trade-off: Faster but less consistent across poses
